About Us

Welcome to my car recommendation project! I am a computer science student with a strong interest in web development and real-world applications of technology. This platform is created as part of my learning journey to help users explore and discover the best car options that suit their budget and preferences.

Whether you're looking for a budget-friendly car, fuel-efficient vehicle, or a model with advanced features, this website simplifies your search. Just enter your budget and filter by fuel type or transmission—our system will show you a list of matching cars with key details and images.

This project is built using HTML, CSS (Bootstrap), Python (Flask), and CSV-based data to provide a simple, user-friendly interface. My goal is to keep improving this project by adding features like advanced filtering, user reviews, and hosting it online for public access.

Thank you for visiting! If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to reach out through the contact section.
